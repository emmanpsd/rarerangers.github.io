About

Rare Rangers is a Social Media Agency, and behind the scenes network of personnel everywhere from Soundcloud producers, all the way up to Graphic Designers and Photographers. We work as a consolidated society behind the scenes of Artist we represent. We facilitate collaboration and connections amongst our community, and have a main purpose of providing top class services for our clients.

Repost Chain Management

We strive to manage and take care of your personal repost chains, we handle all of the hard work that your chain requires. This means we handle all of the email submissions and inquires, but keeping you the host. We insert the tracks into the platform that you run your chain through. We will stay in frequent contact with you as the manager to insure that your chain is running to how you want it. We make it so all you have to worry about is getting people into your chain, while we do the harder work within the management side of your chain. Contact us support@rarerangers.com

Repost Chain

Rare Rangers Repost Chain is fully public and accessible to any producer or artist that would like to join. We have an overall reach of 1 Million followers. We answer and manage the chain submissions efficiently and effectively, insuring engagement on your songs through effective timing. You will be grouped depending on your following, and you will have 1-2 Slots in the chain per month. The thing that separates our chain from other chains, is the pure diversity of the reach, and the differentiation of the overall people in the chain compared to other providers. Our chain is completely free of charge & is constantly growing with new artist.

Promotions

We offer many different promotional offerings for your existing tracks on soundcloud. We utilize various different tools that we use on our own personal tracks to network your music throughout soundcloud effectively. We can further negotiate pricing and the actual promotion we can attach to a specific track or your account/personal brand.

Management

Rare Rangers Management Team works behind the scenes. There will be 2-4 Managers standby at all times. We cover everything from promotion, distribution, and all other social media management. We work behind the scenes of the Artist. For artist we manage we insure that collaborations will be orchestrated through the Rare Rangers community. All promotion and background management will be supported throughout all platforms, kickstarting and growing your artist account exponentially in multiple ways. Being apart of the team gets you full access to the Rare Rangers community, to help grow essential connections. Daily Calls will be hosted by the management to you, we will take notes on everything you want to accomplish, and work on a daily basis on making those dreams true.
